## 🧩 The Problem

Millions of Nigerian informal businesses — suya sellers,
boutique owners, POS agents, keke riders — process
payments through Squad every day but remain invisible
to the formal financial system.

They cannot understand their own business performance,
detect fraud, predict cash flow, or access loans —
despite having real, consistent transaction history.

When they walk into a bank they are told:

> _"You have no financial history."_

**The problem is not lack of transactions.
The problem is lack of intelligence behind them.**

---

## 💡 The Solution

SquadMind transforms raw Squad transaction data into
two powerful outputs:

**Layer 1 — Financial Intelligence**
An AI CFO that gives merchants real-time revenue
insights, fraud detection, cash flow forecasts, and
customer behavior analysis — in plain English or Pidgin.

**Layer 2 — TrustScore (Credit Identity)**
An AI credit score from 0–100 built entirely from
Squad transaction history. No bank statement needed.
Just Squad data — turned into a lender-ready report.

> _One data pipeline. Two powerful outputs._

| API              | Purpose                                   |
